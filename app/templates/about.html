About CropPredict

An academic project leveraging AI to detect crop diseases and support agricultural research

Project Overview

CropPredict is an academic project developed to explore the application of artificial intelligence in agriculture. The project focuses on detecting diseases in crops such as apples, corn, grapes, potatoes, strawberries, and tomatoes using deep learning techniques.

By analyzing leaf images, CropPredict aims to provide accurate disease identification, supporting agricultural research and education. The project includes a web interface for image uploads and a chatbot for interactive guidance on disease management, demonstrating the potential of AI in addressing agricultural challenges.

Technical Approach

CropPredict utilizes convolutional neural networks (CNNs), specifically MobileNetV2 and EfficientNetB3, trained on a dataset of leaf images for crops like apples, corn, and tomatoes. The models classify diseases such as Tomato___Late_blight and Apple___healthy with high accuracy.

The dataset includes thousands of labeled images, enabling robust training and evaluation. The web application, built with Flask, processes image uploads and integrates with a chatbot powered by AI to provide insights on disease prevention and management, showcasing a practical AI pipeline for agriculture.
